Privacy & Data
Your data, handled with care.

What we collect: When you sign up for the HERFORM beta, we collect your name, email address, Instagram handle (if provided), and your responses to our sign-up questions. We may also collect basic usage data as you use the app.

Why we collect it: To manage your beta access, send you product updates, and improve HERFORM based on your feedback. We do not use your data for advertising.
