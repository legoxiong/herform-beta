Your Four Phases
One cycle.
Four training windows.
Most fitness apps ignore your biology. HERFORM is built around it — automatically adjusting your plan as you move through each phase.
01
Menstrual
Days 1–5
Rest, restore, and nourish. Honour your body's need for gentleness. Inflammation is higher — prioritise recovery.
Yoga · Walking · Rest
02
Follicular
Days 6–13
Rising estrogen = rising energy. The perfect window for new challenges and heavy lifting. You're primed.
HIIT · Heavy Lifting
03
Ovulation
Days 14–16
Peak estrogen, peak performance. Your strength and endurance are at their highest. Push your PRs here.
Max Strength · Power
04
Luteal
Days 17–28
Progesterone rises and energy tapers. Shift to moderate intensity, deeper nourishment, and quality sleep.
Pilates · Moderate Strength
